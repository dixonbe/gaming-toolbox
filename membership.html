Term Definitions

Next Term

Status:

Pending/Financial

Bonus time given to new members to have a term end of the following year, ie having a term of 455 days instead of 90 days (Calendar memberhsip only).


Term:

Renewal Period

Status:

Renewal

Number of days before term end that are member can go into renewal (after Membership Billing has been processed).


Term:

Grace Period

Status:

Renewal

Number of days after term end that a member remains in Renewal (Calculated from Term End date).


Term:

Lapsed Period

Status:

Lapsed*

Number of days after term end that a member can still renew even if in a lapsed state (Calculated from Term End date). Lapsed period must be grater than Grace Period to be effective.
